Han Meng Ph.D. candidite, The University of Tokyo, JSPS Research Fellow (DC1)
Publications Education Activities

A doctoral student at the Department of Mechanical Engineeering, the University of Tokyo, advised by Junichiro Shiomi.

My studies focus on improving the figure-of-merit ZT for high-performance thermoelectric materials, as well as the electrical and thermal transport in novel materials for energy applications.
