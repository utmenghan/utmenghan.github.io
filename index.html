Han Meng PhD candidite, The University of Tokyo, JSPS DC1 Research Fellow

I am a doctoral student at Department of Mechanical Engineeering of the University of Tokyo, advised by Junichiro Shiomi.

I am interested in the fundamental study of electrical and thermal transport for high-performance thermoelectric materials.

I am also interested in understanding the thermal transport in novel inorganic materials for energy applications.
